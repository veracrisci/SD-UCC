Why SPD is involved in UCC

Experience-led, not just migrated

The UCC program is a platform migration - but platform decisions are experience decisions. SPD ensures that UCC delivers on its stated ambition: not a like-for-like re-platform of existing limitations, but a migration that leverages AWS Connect's native CX capabilities to materially improve the support experience at launch.

The Intelligent Front Door (IFD) is a design principle, not a separate program. Decisions about how contacts enter, how forms are structured, how intent is classified before routing - these are IFD decisions, whether framed as such or not.

Program Scope

What is UCC / CCaaS?

Customer Service's strategic effort to simplify a fragmented contact-centre landscape on AWS Connect.

  • 01Core channel migration - Live Chat, Voice, Written onto AWS Connect
  • 02Capability development - translation, routing, AI, quality monitoring
  • 03Wider BU expansion - Cars, Rides, Attractions, broader TBU
  • 04Legacy decommissioning - Cisco, Mitel, Intercom shutdown
